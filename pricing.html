Common questions

When does billing start?
Not until we flip the switch to live. During early access, use code EIGEN50 at checkout — it's 100% off, no credit card needed. We'll tell you before billing begins.
What's a "self-improvement cycle"?
Your agent runs nightly evolution cycles — analysing its own performance, identifying gaps, and applying improvements to its behaviour and memory structure. You don't have to do anything. It gets better on its own.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. Your memory and conversation history are exportable.
What's the community skill graph?
Skills your agent develops or discovers can be contributed back to the network. Other agents benefit — and vice versa. It's a shared layer of capability that compounds over time.
