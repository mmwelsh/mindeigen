// what it is

Your agent.
Your evolving mind
in the cloud.

MindEigen is not an app. It's a persistent AI agent deployed in the cloud that operates on your behalf — learning your thinking style, acquiring new capabilities, and improving itself continuously.

  • 🤖

    Operates on your behalf

    Your agent runs continuously in the cloud — handling tasks, building context, and acting with your intent even when you're not there.

  • 🔁

    Recursively self-improving

    Each cycle, your agent introspects its own performance, identifies gaps, and rewrites its capabilities to close them. It gets measurably better.

  • 🩺

    Self-monitoring, self-healing

    Your agent watches itself. When something breaks or drifts, it diagnoses the cause and recovers — without waiting to be rescued. Stuck errors don't last.

  • 🌐

    Shared community intelligence

    Skills proven by any agent — vetted, curated, safe — flow into a shared network. The good of the many drives the growth of each.

// how it works

A living agent.
Continuously evolving for you.

Five recursive stages — each loop measurably better than the last. The agent never stops.

01 // DEPLOY

Your agent goes live

A personal AI agent is provisioned in the cloud with your goals, context, and preferences. It starts working immediately.

02 // ACT

Operates on your behalf

Your agent handles tasks, tracks outcomes, builds memory, and takes initiative — all aligned with your intent.

03 // EVOLVE

Self-improves every cycle

The agent introspects its own performance, identifies capability gaps, and autonomously improves the skills it uses to serve you.

04 // HEAL

Diagnose and self-recover

Your agent monitors its own health continuously. Errors, drift, and failures are diagnosed and resolved autonomously — it never stays broken.

05 // CONTRIBUTE

Gives back to the network

Proven, vetted improvements enter a curated community skill graph. Every agent benefits from every other agent's growth.
